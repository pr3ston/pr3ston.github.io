General Authority Talks

"Joseph Smith was the Lord’s anointed to restore Christ’s Church to the earth.
When he emerged from the grove of trees, he eventually learned four fundamental truths not then taught by the
majority of the contemporary Christian world."

Joseph Smith-Prophet of the Restoration
By Elder Tad R. Callister

In this 2009 talk, Elder Tad R. Callister discusses the life of Joseph Smith Jr. and the importance of the restoration of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. He also discusses the importance of the Book of Mormon and the First Vision. Additionally, he explains several doctrine like how Jesus and Heavenly Father are separate being and yet one in purpose, both Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ have a body of flesh and bones, and that God still communicates to man.

"But the Restoration is not only for those of us who rejoice in it today.
The revelations of the First Vision were not for Joseph Smith alone but are offered
as light and truth for any who “lack wisdom.”

Sharing the Message of the Restoration and the Ressurection
By Elder D. Todd Christofferson

In this 2020 talk, Elder D. Todd Christofferson explains how the message of the Restoration is for everyone, and that it shows how we can ask of God whenever we need wisdom and guidance. He also discusses how it is our duty to share this message and gives several stories as examples. He also discusses the requirements that we must fulfill in order to share this message successfully.

Related D&C Section

Section 8

This section of the Doctrine and Covenants Joseph Smith Jr. is giving Oliver Cowdery a revelation about the Gift of the Holy Ghost. He explains how the the Spirit of Revelation can help in dificult times and can give him guidance. Furthermore, he describes how this is a gift from God and that he must remember to keep the commandments and to stay worthy in order to keep this gift.

How Are These Relevant?

These talks are relevant to the topic of the restoration of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints because they discuss the essential doctrine behind the restoration and first vision. They describe the characteristics of God and also describe how anyone can receive personal revelation from God as long as they are intentful and ask of God. D&C Section 8 is also relevant because it further describes the gift of revelation and how it can help us and give us guidance. All of these things are essential to the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints because they are the foundational knowledge of the Church. They are some examples of the most basic knowledge.
